Jayakwadi Dam

    Jayakwadi is one of the largest earthen dams in Asia. Its height is approx 41.30 m and length of 9,998 m (10 km approx) with total storage capacity 2,909 MCM (million cubic meters) and effective live storage capacity is 2,171 MCM. The total catchment area of dam is 21,750 km2. There are total 27 water gates for the dam.
    LOCATION: Aurangabad, Maharashtra, India
