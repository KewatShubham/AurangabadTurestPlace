Aurangabad Caves

    Aurangabad caves will take some time for you to visit it entirely. Beautiful surroundings with lush greenery and ancient rock-cut caves welcome you to the magnificence of Maharashtra. Caves are divided into three separate groups depending on their location as the First group: Cave 1 to Cave 5, Second group: Cave 6 to Cave 9 and Third group: Cave10 to Cave 12. The first two caves have a distance of 500 metres between them, and the third one is slightly further to the east. They are further also known by their division into three main groups that are western group caves and eastern group caves and a third group which is further the east of the second group.A true architectural wonder with a deep history is attached to these caves
